About Me

I am a Ph.D. researcher in Mechanical Engineering at UCLA, specializing in tactile sensing, perception, and human–robot interaction. My work focuses on designing tactile sensors and integrating them with robotic manipulators and haptic feedback systems to create closed-loop, multimodal interaction frameworks. These systems enable robots to manipulate objects with greater dexterity and provide intuitive feedback to human operators.

I have experience developing integrated robotic platforms, leading interdisciplinary collaborations, and mentoring students. My projects span from microfluidic-based tactile sensors to full human–robot teleoperation systems. I am passionate about advancing robotic technologies that enhance perception, adaptability, and collaboration between humans and machines in real-world environments.
