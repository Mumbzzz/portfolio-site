About Me

I am a Ph.D. researcher in Mechanical Engineering at UCLA, specializing in tactile sensing, perception, and human-robot interaction. My work focuses on developing novel sensors and haptic systems that enable robots to manipulate objects with greater dexterity and provide intuitive feedback to human operators.

I have experience leading interdisciplinary research collaborations, mentoring students, and contributing to projects ranging from microfluidics-based sensors to immersive teleoperation systems. I am passionate about advancing robotics technologies that improve autonomy, dexterity, and usability in real-world applications.
