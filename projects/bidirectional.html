Bidirectional Human-Robot System with Touch (2025)

This project explores the design of haptic feedback systems to enable more intuitive teleoperation and communication between humans and robots. The goal was to create a two-way interface that allows operators not only to control robotic systems but also to “feel” the environment through tactile signals.

The system was tested on robotic platforms and integrated with tactile sensors developed as part of my doctoral research at UCLA.
